Helping the well known feel less alone

There's an emerging generation of Gen Z Christian athletes, rappers, actors and singers who are taking over the sports & entertainment industry.

We're creating a private discipleship community to help them carry the weight of their crown like Jesus.

This movement isn’t about fame—it’s about faith, purpose, and healing. Whether you're on stage, on set, or in the studio, you're not alone. We're building something sacred for those who carry influence and feel the weight of it.
